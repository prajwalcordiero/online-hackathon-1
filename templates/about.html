ℹ️ About the Data-Driven Retail Solution

This application demonstrates a data-driven approach to solving common challenges faced by Nordic retailers: fluctuating demand, static pricing, and operational costs.

Our Approach

  • Demand Forecasting: We analyze historical sales data, segmenting it by region and product, to predict future inventory needs and prevent both overstocking (waste) and understocking (lost sales).
  • Dynamic Pricing: We move beyond manager 'gut feeling' by suggesting real-time price adjustments based on the demand forecast, current stock levels, and competitor pricing (simulated).
  • Technology Stack: The application uses **Python (Flask)** for the backend, **Pandas** for high-speed data processing, and **HTML/Bootstrap** for a responsive user interface.

This proof-of-concept lays the foundation for integrating complex machine learning models to maximize profitability and operational efficiency.
